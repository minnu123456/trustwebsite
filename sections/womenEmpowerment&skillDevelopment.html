Women Empowerment & Skill Development
Our trust is committed to empowering women by providing skill development, financial independence, and safety training. Through vocational programs and mentorship, we enable women to become confident, self-reliant, and leaders in their communities.

Vocational Training Centres

Tailoring, agriculture, digital skills, and more are taught to women through hands-on training programs, enabling sustainable livelihoods.

Entrepreneurship Support

We provide funding, mentorship, and business guidance to women entrepreneurs, helping them start and grow their own enterprises successfully.

Self-Defence & Financial Independence

Training programs for self-defence, personal safety, and financial management equip women with confidence and independence in daily life.

Leadership & Community Participation

Workshops and community programs encourage women to take active leadership roles in local governance, social initiatives, and educational outreach.
